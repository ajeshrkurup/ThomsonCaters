About Us

Thomson Caterers was established in the year of 2006 by a group of friends. We have gained a reputation as one of the leaders in the catering industry for prompt service, superior quality and competitive pricing. We are a full service catering company providing delicious food, professional service and experienced coordination. We furnish all the serving pieces needed to create a lush and inviting presentation. Our style of events range from drop off and delivery service with our delicious home – style selections to custom special menus and service for all events needs, trimmed and tailored just for you. The presentation of our menus and quality of our service are sure to match your corporate objectives. Menus and packages may be specially designed for your organization. Thomson offers indoor and outdoor catering in and out of Kerala, with full kitchen set up and service for Breakfast, Lunch, Hi- Tea & Dinner. Chicken corner, Nadan thattukada, Dessert corner and Fruit stall are our specialities. We conduct services for all types of events like betrothal, marriage celebration, baptism, dine-n-dance Parties, barbeque night, cocktail Parties, christmas & new year functions, parties for Foreign Delegates etc…Our major services are catering, decoration, video & photography, light & sound, music and entertainment, arranging of auditoriums & halls, security services, wedding choreography etc. We take care of the event so that you can relax and enjoy your occasion without any hassles.
